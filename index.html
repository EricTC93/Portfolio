About Me

I'm a lover of games, math, logic, and coding and I believe those interests go together very well. I went from applied math major to a logic-loving coder that wouldn’t mind spending many hours solving problems or working on vast projects.

Math has been my favorite subject since forever and I enjoy the many applications of it. I even volunteered as a math tutor and I was able to help others learn the subject. I enjoyed this experience and I wouldn't mind teaching others in subjects I know very well.

Coding has become another favorite subject of mine that I'm currently pursuing a career in, more specifically in web development. From someone who loves games and logic puzzles, to figure out and create the logic for different types of projects gives me a satisfying feeling. This is why I love working in the back end of web development, figuring out and creating the logic for different kinds of applications is what gives me the most satisfaction.

This is probably the reason why I enjoy languages like node.js, Javascript, and C++. I would love to work back-end and create a complex website that’s useful or websites for others to enjoy.
